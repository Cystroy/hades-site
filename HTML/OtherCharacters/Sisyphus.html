Sisyphus

Sisyphus was a former king who was sentenced to push a boulder up a hill for eternity. The boulder however, keeps rolling back down. Sisyphus gifts Zagreus a variety of items to help him escape.

Rewards

Item Description

Health

Health heals you and increases your HP

Darkness

 Darkness is currency used to upgrade Zagreus via the Mirror of Night.

Obols

 Obols is currency used to buy items from Charon's shop during an escape attempt.
