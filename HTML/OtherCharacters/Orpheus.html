Orpheus

Orpheus is the Court Musician of the House of Hades. When he was alive, his wife Eurydice died and he trespassed into the Underworld to try and bring her back to the surface. He has a contract which makes him unable to go near Eurydice in the Underworld. He occasionally sings and plays his harp in the House of Hades.
