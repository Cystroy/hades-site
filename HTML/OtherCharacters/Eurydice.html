Eurydice

Eurydice is a nymph that died from a snake bite. She is the former wife of Orpheus, who went down to the Underworld to try and get her back to the surface. Eurydice resides in a home in Asphodel, where she spends her time cooking. She helps Zagreus escape by giving him items that give positve effects to his boons.
