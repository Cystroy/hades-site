What is Hades?

Hades is an action video game where players control the Greek mythology character Zagreus, who is the son of Hades.

What is the story?

Zagreus is the son of Hades, the King of Death. In a flashback, Zagreus finds out that he is the son of Persephone, the Goddess of Vegetation. All his life he thought his mother was Nyx, the Night Incarnate. Upon finding out about this information, Zagreus sets out to escape the House of Hades and venture outside to find his mother, with the help of the Champions of Olympius.
