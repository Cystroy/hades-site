Aphrodite

Poseidon is the God of the Sea and a brother of Zeus, Hades and Demeter. He is very brash and blunt. He wields his trident and has control over water and uses that power to create devestating floods.

Boons

Poseidon grants a myriad of abilities that provide Zagreus the ability to control water. These powers are destrutive and are representative of he violent potential of the oceans.

Boon Description

Heartbreak Strike

 Attack deals more damage and can give enemies Weak.

Heartbreak Flourish

 Special does more damage and gives enemies Weak.

Crush Shot

 Cast becomes a wide and short-range blast and causes Weak.

Passion Flare

 Cast hurts enemies around Zagreus and causes Weak.

Passion Dash

 Dash hurts enemies where Zagreus ends up and causes Weak.

Aphrodite's Aid

 Call shoots a homing projectile that causes Charm.

Dying Lament

 Slain enemies deal damage to close enemies and cause Weak.

Wave of Despair

 Hurt and give foes Weak when damaged.

Different League

 Endure some attacks from close enemies.

Life Affirmation

 Chamber rewards that give Health are more valuable.

Empty Inside

 Weak lasts longer.

Sweet Surrender

 Enemies with Weak have less endurance from damage.

Broken Resolve

 Weak effects are more powerful.

Blown Kiss

 Cast shoots farther and is more powerful when shot to an undamaged enemy.

Unhealthy Fixation

 Weak has a 15% chance to Charm enemies.

Curse of Longing

 Doom repeatedly hits Weak enemies.

Heart Rend

 Critical effects do more damage to Weak enemies.

Parting Shot

 Cast gets bonus damage when attacking behind enemies.

Cold Embrace

 Cast crystal shoots out its beam straight at Zagreus for 4 seconds.

Low Tolerance

 Hangover effects stack more against enemies with Weak.

Sweet Nectar

 Poms of Power found are more effective.

Smoldering Air

 Call charges automatically, but is now capped up to 25% of the gauge.

Aphrodite's Effects

Aphrodite's boons give Zagreus abilities to weaken enemy attacks with his attacks.

Weak

Weak makes enemies do less damage. Enemies that are Weak can become Charmed. Charmed enemies attack each other.

Charm

Ruptured enemies take damage while they move.
