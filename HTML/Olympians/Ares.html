Ares

Ares is the God of War and a son of Zeus and Hera. He is violent and warlike. Being the God of War, he is entertained by mortals killing each other in wars.

Boons

Boon Description

Curse of Agony

 Attack deals more damage and inflicts Doom on enemies.

Curse of Pain

 Special deals more damage and inflicts Doom on enemies.

Slicing Shot

 Cast becomes a Blade Rift that travels forward.

Slicing Flare

 Cast becomes a large Blade Rift that travels forward (Aspect of Beowulf).

Blade Dash

 Dash creates a Blade Rift.

Ares' Aid

 Call transforms you into a Blade Rift that is impervious for 1.2 seconds.

Curse of Vengeance

 When hit by an enemy, they are inflicted with Doom.

Urge to Kill

 Increase the damage of your Attack, Special and Cast.

Battle Rage

 After you kill an enemy, the strength of your next Attack or Special is increased.

Blood Frenzy

 After a Death Defiance is used, you do increased damage.

Black Metal

 Blade Rift effects become wider.

Engulfing Vortex

 Duration of Blade Rift effects become longer, and they will now pull enemies in .

Dire Misfortune

 Doom effects now stack damage.

Impending Doom

 Doom effects will have increased damage, triggering after a 0.5 second delay.

Vicious Cycle

 Blade Rift now does consecutive damage per hit.

Curse of Longing

 Doom effects will persistently damage Weakened enemies.

Hunting Blades

 Cast creates a faster Blade Rift that seeks enemies.

Merciful End

 Deflecting attacks will trigger Doom on enemies.

Freezing Vortex

 Cast becomes smaller and slower but inflicts Chill

Curse of Nausea

 Hangovers will damage enemies faster.

Curse of Drowning

 Cast becomes a pulsating force that damages surrounding enemies.

Vengeful Mood

 Revenge effects will sometimes trigger without you taking damage.

Ares' Effects

Ares' boons give Zagreus abilities to summon a Blade Rift that continously hits enemies.

Blade Rift

Blade Rift is a spinning projectile that repeatedly hurts enemies.

Doom

Doom is a status effect for enemies that causes them to take a great amount of damage a while after being inflicted with Doom.
