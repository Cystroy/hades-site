Zeus

Zeus is the God of Thunder and the father to many Gods and a brother of Poseidon, Demeter, and Hades. He is longing to make amends with his brother Hades. Zeus is afraid of Demeter finding out the truth of her daughter Persephone because of his involvement in her "disappearence" from Olympus.

Thunder Flourish

 Special can strike opponents with lightning.

Electric Shot

 Cast becomes a burst of lightning that ricochets between enemies.

Thunder Flare

 Special deals more damage and can deflect attacks.

Thunder Dash

 Call makes nearby enemies struck by lightning again and again for 1.5 seconds.

Double Strike

Static Discharge

Zeus' Effects

Zeus' boons give Zagreus abilities to cause a lightning bolt to hurt enemies with his attacks.

Chain-lightning attacks enemies by bouncing from enemy to enemy. Enemies that are hit by a bolt can become Jolted.

Jolted

Jolted enemies take damage when they attack.
