Who are the Olympians?

The Olympians are gods that reside on Mount Olympus. They aid Zagreus by giving him boons when Zagreus contacts them while attempting to escape the House. The Olympians think Zagreus is trying to escape to the surface to get to them, unknowing that he is escaping to find his birth mother Persephone.
