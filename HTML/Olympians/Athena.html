Athena

Poseidon is the God of the Sea and a brother of Zeus, Hades and Demeter. He is very brash and blunt. He wields his trident and has control over water and uses that power to create devestating floods.

Boons

Poseidon grants a myriad of abilities that provide Zagreus the ability to control water. These powers are destrutive and are representative of he violent potential of the oceans.

Boon Description

Divine Strike

 Attack deals more damage and can deflect attacks.

Divine Flourish

 Special deals more damage and can deflect attacks.

Phalanx Shot

 Cast deals damage in a small area and can deflect attacks.

Phalanx Flare

 Cast deals damage in a small area and can deflect attacks.

Divine Dash

 Dash can deal damage and deflect attacks.

Athena's Aid

 Call makes Zagreus Invulnerable and deflect attacks for 1.5/1.65/1.8/1.95 seconds.

Holy Shield

 Deal damage and deflect for a while after taking damage.

Bronze Skin

 Reduced damage from enemy attacks.

Sure Footing

 Reduced damage from Traps.

Proud Bearing

 God Guage is partly full for the start of each Encounter.

Blinding Flash

 Attacks that deflect now makes foes Exposed for 5 seconds.

Brilliant Riposte

 Do more damage when deflecting enemy attacks.

Deathless Stand

 Death Defiance makes Zagreus Impervious longer and gives one Death Defiance.

Last Stand

 Death Defiance replenishes more health now and gives one Death Defiance.

Divine Protection

 Zagreus is given a barrier that blocks attacks.

Parting Shot

 Cast gets bonus damage when attacking behind enemies.

Merciful End

 Attacks that deflect instantly cause Doom.

Deadly Reversal

 After deflecting an attack, gain a 20% chance to do critical damage.

Stubborn Roots

 If Zagreus has no Death Defiance, he regenerates one health every 0.8 seconds.

Calculated Risk

 Enemies' projectiles are slowed down.

Unshakable Mettle

 Zagreus can't be stunned and can endure some damage from Bosses.

Lightning Phalanx

 Phalanx Shot cast now bounces from foe to foe.

Athena's Effects

Athena's boons give Zagreus abilities to deflect enemy attacks with his attacks.

Deflect

Deflect reflects enemy attacks. Enemies that are hit by a deflected attack can become Exposed.

Exposed

Ruptured enemies take damage while they move.
