The Furies

The Furies are three sisters that are the first boss Zagreus encounters during an escape attempt. Megaera, Alecto, or Tisiphone, two of them, or all three of them may engange in battle with him.

Megaera

Megaera is one of the three sisters. She and Zagreus are former lovers. Zagreus makes attempts to make ammends with her throughout the game.

Alecto

Alecto is one of the three sisters. She is shown to be extremely violent.

Tisiphone

Tisiphone is one of the three sisters. She is known as the Tormentor of Murder. When enganging in coversation with her, she calls Zagreus "Murderer" before attempting to learn how to say his name.
